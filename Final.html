This is My Final Project!

This little game will simply allow an user to do a self test to see wether they are the type of person to like winter or summer, if they are extroverts or introverts, or if they are optimistic or pessimistic.

To play, just click on the start button, and then pick which selection you want to play from. Then just click on the diferrent options, when you are done, click "Check" and see your results!
